About US

I'm yogi abhilaksh tyagi. I'm a certified yoga trainer & a yoga instructor. I've completed my Bachelor's ( BSC ) with Yoga & my Post Graduation with ( Yogic Science ). I've 5 years of experience of self-practice and 1 years of experience of teaching yoga.I've Accupracture points certificate. Expert in yoga for flexibility, yoga for women's & men, yoga for holistic living, yoga for kids, yoga for senior citizens etc. My teaching style is the combination of indian traditional yoga practice and modern approach. I always follow vinyasa flow in which strength, flexibility and stability of each and every body movement are emphasized together with proper alignment, breathing and focusing points. I hold that these techniques can create a balance between each practitioners body, mind and soul. These days I'm teaching yoga and with long experience of solving different problems that students usually encounter during their practice, I am running various courses and techniques, workshops to share my knowledge about yoga, anatomy and alignments with the hope of assisting more and more practitioners in increasing awareness about themselves and learned yoga.
